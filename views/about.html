About Salama Blue Lodge

Discover our story and commitment to excellence

Your Comfort Haven in Lusaka

Welcome to Salama Blue Lodge, where spacious comfort meets warm Zambian hospitality. Located in Salama Park, 10101 Lusaka, our lodge is conveniently situated just 9 km from Kenneth Kaunda International Airport and minutes from the city center.

Perfect for business travelers, holidaymakers, and anyone seeking a peaceful, well-appointed stay. Relax on your private balcony with city views, enjoy our hearty breakfast selections, and take advantage of easy access to Lusaka's top attractions. Our name "Salama" means "peace" in Swahili, reflecting our commitment to providing a serene experience amidst the vibrant energy of Lusaka.

Our Founder & CEO

Fikreselassie Kahssay

Fikreselassie Kahssay is an Australian entrepreneur with a proven track record in strategic investments across Africa. His portfolio spans major projects in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia; Nairobi, Kenya; and the Tigray region, with a strong focus on real estate and sustainable development.

In 2021, he brought his expertise to Zambia, establishing Segen Investment Ltd under an Investor Licence Category D. Since then, he has led the successful development of key commercial and hospitality properties, including Garden Shops, Kabwata Shopping Complex, and Salama Blue Lodge.

Driven by a vision to create lasting value, Mr. Kahssay combines innovative thinking with a deep understanding of local markets. His leadership continues to shape Segen Investment Ltd as a trusted name in property development and a catalyst for economic growth in the region.
